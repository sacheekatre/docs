This guide walks you through everything you need to hit the ground running on Closd. By the end, you will have a live transaction project, your team invited, and your first checklist item ready to track — no prior experience with the platform required.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Go to [app.closd.com](https://app.closd.com) and click **Sign Up**. Enter your professional email address and choose a secure password, then check your inbox for a verification email. Click the confirmation link to activate your account and access the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Your Profile">
    After logging in, open the account menu in the top-right corner and select **Profile Settings**. Enter your full name, organization, and role (for example, "Associate" or "Head of M&A"). You can also upload a profile picture to help your colleagues identify you across shared projects.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your First Project">
    From your dashboard, click **New Project**. Give your project a clear, descriptive name — for example, *Acquisition of Acme Corp*. Select the transaction type that best matches your deal (M&A, Real Estate, Private Equity, Banking, or Other), then click **Create**. Your project workspace opens immediately, ready to be configured.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite Your Team">
    Navigate to the **Members** tab inside your project and click **Invite**. Enter the email addresses of your colleagues and any external parties such as counterparties or advisors. Assign each person an appropriate role:
    - **Admin** — full control over the project, including settings and member management
    - **Editor** — can upload documents, complete checklist items, and collaborate actively
    - **Viewer** — read-only access, ideal for clients or observers

    Invitees receive an email with a direct link to join the project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your First Checklist Item">
    Open the **Checklist** tab within your project. Click **Add Item** and enter a descriptive task name — for example, *Draft Share Purchase Agreement*. Assign the item to a team member, set a due date, and click **Save**. Your checklist is now live and will update in real time as your team marks items complete.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Save time on recurring transaction types by starting from a template instead of building your checklist from scratch. On the **New Project** screen, select **Use a Template** to load a pre-built checklist structure tailored to your deal type.
</Tip>
