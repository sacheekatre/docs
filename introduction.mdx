Welcome to Closd, the cloud-based legal transaction management platform built for lawyers, in-house counsel, and dealmakers. Closd brings every stage of your deal into a single, secure workspace — from initial due diligence through to post-closing documentation — so your team spends less time chasing documents and more time closing deals.
