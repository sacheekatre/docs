Legal transactions involve some of the most sensitive documents your organisation will ever handle — from M&A term sheets to real estate contracts and financing agreements. Closd is purpose-built for this environment: enterprise-grade security is not an add-on but a foundational design principle. This page explains the technical and organisational measures Closd uses to protect your data, ensure regulatory compliance, and give your team and clients the confidence to transact digitally.

## Data Residency

Closd stores all customer data — documents, project metadata, audit trails, and archived files — exclusively on servers located within the European Union. Your data is never transferred outside the EU. This is a critical assurance for law firms, in-house legal teams, and financial institutions subject to EU data protection rules, cross-border transfer restrictions, and client confidentiality obligations. You always know exactly where your data lives.

## GDPR Compliance

Closd is fully compliant with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Acting as a data processor on your behalf, Closd has implemented all required technical and organisational measures to safeguard personal data processed through the platform. You retain full control over the personal data stored in your projects — including the right to access, correct, and request deletion of that data at any time. A Data Processing Agreement (DPA) is available for enterprise customers upon request.

## Encryption

All data transmitted to and from Closd is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher, preventing interception during upload, download, and collaboration. Data stored on Closd's servers is encrypted at rest using AES-256, one of the strongest encryption standards available. This two-layer encryption approach means your confidential documents are protected whether they are moving across the internet or sitting in storage.

## Access Controls

Closd gives administrators fine-grained control over who can see, edit, and act on every document and project.

- **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):** MFA is available for all user accounts, adding a critical second layer of protection beyond passwords.
- **Role-Based Permissions:** Assign roles at the project level to control what each participant — internal team members, external advisors, counterparties — can access and do.
- **Per-Document Permissions:** In the data room, permissions can be set at the individual document or folder level, ensuring that sensitive sub-sets of a deal are visible only to the right parties.
- **Session Management:** Inactive sessions are automatically timed out to reduce the risk of unauthorised access from unattended devices.
- **Watermarking:** Downloaded documents in the data room can be automatically watermarked with the viewer's identity, deterring unauthorised distribution.

## Penetration Testing and Security Audits

Closd undergoes regular independent security assessments, including penetration tests conducted by qualified third-party security firms. These tests proactively identify and remediate vulnerabilities before they can be exploited. As part of the LexisNexis and RELX Group ecosystem, Closd also benefits from the broader enterprise security governance, risk, and compliance programmes maintained by its parent organisation.

## Certifications

Closd operates within a framework of recognised security and trust certifications. Arkhineo, Closd's long-term electronic archiving partner, holds **NF Z42-013** certification — the French standard for trusted digital archiving — and operates in compliance with **eIDAS** trust service requirements, providing strong legal probative value for archived documents.

## Business Continuity and Availability

Closd is built on redundant cloud infrastructure with automatic failover to ensure your projects remain accessible when you need them most — including during critical closing days. Key continuity measures include:

- **Redundant Infrastructure:** Multiple availability zones with automatic failover prevent single points of failure.
- **Daily Backups:** Data is backed up daily, with recovery procedures regularly tested.
- **SLA Commitments:** Closd provides uptime service level agreements for enterprise customers. Contact your account manager for current SLA terms.

<Tip>
  Enable two-factor authentication (2FA / MFA) for every team member working on sensitive transactions. This single step dramatically reduces the risk of unauthorised account access, even if a user's password is compromised. Your Closd administrator can enforce MFA as a requirement across all users in your organisation.
</Tip>
