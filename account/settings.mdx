Your Closd account settings let you control how your profile appears to others, how the platform communicates with you, and how your account is secured. Keeping these settings up to date ensures a smooth experience for you and your collaborators across every transaction.

To access your settings, click your profile icon in the top-right corner of any Closd page and select **Account Settings**.

---

## Profile Settings

Your profile is visible to everyone you collaborate with in Closd, including counterparties and external advisors. Keeping it accurate builds trust and ensures that signature requests and communications are clearly attributed to the right person.

In the **Profile** section, you can update:

- **Full name** — Your first and last name as it should appear on documents and communications.
- **Title and role** — Your professional title (for example, *Partner*, *Senior Associate*, or *General Counsel*).
- **Organization** — The name of your firm or company.
- **Profile photo** — Upload a professional headshot to help teammates identify you at a glance.
- **Contact information** — Your phone number and any other contact details you want to make available to project collaborators.

After making changes, click **Save** to apply them.

---

## Notification Settings

Closd sends email notifications to keep you informed of activity across your projects. You can tailor these notifications so you receive the updates that matter to you without inbox clutter.

In the **Notifications** section, you can turn each of the following on or off:

- **Document uploads** — Get notified when a new document is added to a project you're involved in.
- **Checklist updates** — Receive alerts when checklist items are completed, assigned, or flagged.
- **Signature requests** — Be notified immediately when a document is sent to you for signature.
- **Due date reminders** — Receive advance reminders before checklist item deadlines or project milestones.

Toggle each notification type using the switch next to it, then click **Save Preferences** to confirm your choices.

<Info>
  Signature request notifications are turned on by default and cannot be fully disabled, as they are required for you to act on time-sensitive signing tasks.
</Info>

## Language & Regional Settings

Closd is available in multiple languages, making it easy for international deal teams to work in their preferred language. Each user can set their own language independently — your choice does not affect what your colleagues see.

To change the interface language:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Account Settings">
    Click your profile icon in the top-right corner and select **Account Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Language & Region">
    Select the **Language & Region** tab from the settings menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your language">
    Select your preferred language from the dropdown list of available options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save**. The Closd interface refreshes automatically in your chosen language.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Security Settings

Protecting access to your Closd account is especially important given the sensitivity of the legal documents and transaction data it contains. Use the **Security** section of your account settings to manage your credentials and active sessions.

**Change your password**

Click **Change Password**, enter your current password, then enter and confirm your new password. Click **Save** to update it.

**Two-factor authentication (2FA)**

Two-factor authentication adds a second verification step when you log in, requiring both your password and a one-time code from an authentication app on your phone. To enable 2FA:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Security Settings">
    Navigate to **Account Settings** and select the **Security** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable two-factor authentication">
    Click **Enable 2FA** and follow the on-screen instructions. You will be asked to scan a QR code with an authenticator app such as Google Authenticator or Microsoft Authenticator.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your backup codes">
    Closd provides a set of one-time backup codes during setup. Store these in a safe place — you will need them if you ever lose access to your authentication app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To disable 2FA, return to the **Security** tab, click **Disable 2FA**, and confirm your password.

**Active sessions**

The **Active Sessions** panel shows every device and browser currently logged in to your account. If you see a session you don't recognize, click **Revoke** next to it to log that device out immediately. To log out of all devices at once, click **Revoke All Sessions**.

<Tip>
  Enable two-factor authentication on your account, especially if you regularly work on sensitive M&A, financing, or real estate transactions. It significantly reduces the risk of unauthorized access even if your password is ever compromised.
</Tip>
