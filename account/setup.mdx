Before you can start managing transactions on Closd, you need to create and configure your account. This guide walks you through signing up, verifying your identity, and completing your professional profile so your colleagues and counterparties can identify you correctly throughout your projects.

## Creating Your Account

Follow these steps to get your Closd account up and running.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Closd sign-up page">
    Open your browser and navigate to [app.closd.com](https://app.closd.com). Click **Sign Up** to begin creating your account. Enter your professional email address and choose a strong password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email address">
    After submitting your details, Closd sends a verification email to the address you provided. Open that email and click **Verify my email** to confirm your account. Check your spam or junk folder if the email doesn't arrive within a few minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your professional profile">
    Once verified, you are prompted to fill in your profile details. Enter your full name, firm or organization name, and your role or title (for example, *Associate*, *In-house Counsel*, or *Notary*). This information appears on documents, signature requests, and project communications, so make sure it accurately reflects your professional identity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your notification preferences">
    Choose how you want Closd to keep you informed. You can opt in to email notifications for events such as new document uploads, checklist updates, incoming signature requests, and approaching due dates. You can always adjust these preferences later in your account settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Closd accounts are tied to your organization's subscription. If you are unable to complete the sign-up process, contact your Closd administrator — they may need to add you to your organization's account before you can proceed.
</Note>

---

## Logging In

Once your account is set up, navigate to [app.closd.com](https://app.closd.com) and enter your email address and password to log in.

If your organization has configured **Single Sign-On (SSO)**, you may see an option to log in through your company's identity provider instead. Contact your IT administrator or Closd account admin to find out whether SSO is available to you.

---

## Resetting Your Password

If you forget your password, you can reset it at any time without contacting support.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the login page">
    Navigate to [app.closd.com](https://app.closd.com) and click **Forgot your password?** below the login form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Type in the email address associated with your Closd account and click **Send reset link**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow the link in your email">
    Open the password reset email from Closd and click the secure link inside. You will be prompted to create a new password. Once saved, you can log in immediately with your new credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Choose a password that is at least 12 characters long and includes a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. For an extra layer of protection on sensitive transactions, enable two-factor authentication (2FA) in your **Security Settings** after logging in.
</Tip>
