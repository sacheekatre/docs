A Virtual Data Room (VDR) is a secure, controlled environment where confidential documents are shared with counterparties during a transaction — typically for due diligence in M&A, real estate, or financing deals. Closd's Data Room is purpose-built for legal professionals: it gives you granular control over who sees what, logs every action taken, and protects document integrity through watermarking — all within the same platform you use to manage your transaction from signing to closing.

## Setting Up a Data Room

Your data room lives inside a Closd project — it is not a standalone module. This means your deal workspace, signatories, and data room are all connected, eliminating the need to juggle multiple platforms during a transaction.

To enable the data room within a project:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    Log in to [app.closd.com](https://app.closd.com) and navigate to the project for which you want to run due diligence.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Modules">
    In the project sidebar, select **Modules** and locate the **Data Room** option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable the Data Room">
    Toggle the Data Room module on. The Data Room tab will immediately appear in your project navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure settings before inviting participants">
    Before adding buyers or advisors, review your security settings — including watermarking and participant confidentiality — under **Data Room Settings**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Index and number your folders (e.g. "1. Corporate", "2. Financial", "3. Legal") before inviting any buyers. A well-organised index gives counterparties a professional first impression and reduces the volume of early Q&A questions about where to find documents.
</Tip>

## Data Room vs. Project Documents

Closd projects include a standard document area for internal collaboration — uploading drafts, collecting signatures, and sharing files with your own team. The Data Room is a distinct, hardened environment designed for external disclosure to counterparties.

| Feature | Project Documents | Data Room |
|---|---|---|
| Granular per-user permissions | Limited | ✓ |
| Participant confidentiality | ✗ | ✓ |
| Watermarking on download | ✗ | ✓ |
| Full activity audit log | ✗ | ✓ |
| Q&A workflow | ✗ | ✓ |

Use the standard document area for internal drafts and team collaboration. Switch to the Data Room the moment you are ready to disclose confidential materials to buyers, investors, or their advisors.

<Note>
  Closd stores all data on European servers and complies with EU data protection regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). If your deal involves parties subject to specific data residency requirements, contact your Closd administrator to confirm the applicable storage configuration before opening the data room to participants.
</Note>
